Privacy Policy

LeadFlow AI - How We Protect Your Data

Last Updated: February 24, 2026
Effective Date: February 24, 2026
TLDR: We collect minimal data (email, payment info, lead preferences). We never sell your data. We use industry-standard encryption. You can export or delete your data anytime.

1. Introduction

UpGo Web AI Agency ("we", "our", "us") operates LeadFlow AI. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

By using LeadFlow AI, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information You Provide

Data Type Purpose Required?
Email address Account creation, notifications, support Yes
Payment information Billing (processed by Stripe) Yes (paid plans)
Company name Personalization, reporting No
Phone number Support, urgent notifications No
Lead preferences Keyword targeting, industry filters Yes

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent, clicks
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address
  • Cookies: Session cookies for authentication, analytics cookies for improvement
  • Lead Interactions: Which leads you viewed, contacted, or marked as irrelevant

2.3 Third-Party Data

We collect publicly available data from platforms like Reddit, LinkedIn, and Twitter. This data is:

  • Already publicly accessible (not private or scraped)
  • Obtained through official APIs or public RSS feeds
  • Used solely to identify business opportunities matching your criteria
  • Not stored permanently (only leads matching your filters are saved)

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Service Delivery

  • Monitor platforms for leads matching your criteria
  • Score and rank leads based on AI algorithms
  • Send real-time notifications of high-quality opportunities
  • Provide response templates and engagement suggestions

3.2 Account Management

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Process payments and send billing notifications
  • Provide customer support
  • Send important service updates

3.3 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features
  • Train AI models for better lead scoring
  • Debug technical issues
  • Develop new features based on user behavior

3.4 Marketing (with consent)

  • Send product updates and tips (you can unsubscribe)
  • Share case studies and success stories (anonymized)
  • Offer promotions for beta customers

4. How We Share Your Information

We NEVER sell your data. We only share information as described below.

4.1 Service Providers

We share data with trusted third parties who help us operate:

  • Stripe: Payment processing (PCI-DSS compliant)
  • AWS: Cloud hosting and data storage
  • SendGrid: Email delivery
  • Slack: Customer support and notifications (Agency tier)

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to:

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Prevent fraud or abuse
  • Cooperate with law enforcement

4.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale, your information may be transferred. We will notify you before your data is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

4.4 Aggregated Data

We may share anonymized, aggregated statistics (e.g., "LeadFlow AI users see an average of 25 leads/week"). This data cannot identify you personally.

5. Data Security

5.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption: TLS/SSL for data in transit, AES-256 for data at rest
  • Access Controls: Role-based permissions, multi-factor authentication
  • Regular Audits: Quarterly security reviews and penetration testing
  • Secure Infrastructure: AWS with SOC 2 Type II compliance

5.2 Your Responsibility

  • Keep your password secure (use a password manager)
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
  • Report suspicious activity immediately
  • Log out from shared devices

6. Your Privacy Rights

6.1 Access and Portability

You have the right to:

  • View all data we have about you
  • Export your data in JSON or CSV format
  • Request a copy of your lead history

6.2 Correction and Update

You can update your information anytime through account settings or by contacting support.

6.3 Deletion ("Right to be Forgotten")

You can request account deletion anytime. We will:

  • Delete your personal information within 30 days
  • Retain anonymized analytics for up to 90 days
  • Keep financial records for 7 years (legal requirement)

6.4 Opt-Out of Marketing

  • Click "Unsubscribe" in any marketing email
  • Adjust notification preferences in account settings
  • Email us at banmart@gmail.com

6.5 California Residents (CCPA)

California users have additional rights:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know if we sell or share data (we don't)
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Opt-out of data sales (not applicable - we don't sell data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising rights

6.6 EU Residents (GDPR)

EU users have rights under GDPR:

  • Right to access
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object
  • Rights related to automated decision-making
